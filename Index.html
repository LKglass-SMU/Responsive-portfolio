About Me

After 20 years of working for the Department of Defense (DoD), Aerospace (Engineering, Contract Pricing, Scheduling and Acquisitions), NASA/Jet Propulsion Laboratory (JPL) Space Flight Program, Los Angeles Air-Force Base and The Boeing Company, I have retired with great experience. Having excellent soft skills in Program Management, Team Building and Project Leadership I decided with unwavering drive and ambition to become part of the Digital Revolution and become a web developer.

Currently attending SMU Coding Full Stack Program to enhance my Technical Skill, learning Browser Based Technologies (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, JQuery, React.js, Bootstrap), API Interactions (AJAX, JSON, Python, C#, Java) and Git/Github I plan to redirect my career opportunities.
