Kendra Glass

After a combined 20 years of working for NASA/Jet Propulsion Laboratory (JPL), Space Flight Program Department of Defense (DoD), Aerospace (Engineering), LA Air-Force Base and The Boeing Company, I have retired with a wealth of knowledge and experience. Excellent soft skills in Program Management, Team Building and Project Leadership I decided with unwavering drive and ambition to become part of the Digital Revolution and lean web developing.

Currently attending SMU Coding Full Stack Program to enhance the necessary Technical Skill needed to help with this new interest I am learning Browser Based Technologies (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, JQuery, React.js, Bootstrap), API Interactions (AJAX, JSON, Python, C#, Java) and how to utilize Git/Github.

Although my future plans do not include seeking employment I am looking forward to sharing my experience with inner-city youth to help better prepare them in the event the path of coding is in their future.
